iTunes Player.

Where all your favorite stuff is just a click away.

The iTunes player keeps all your music, apps, lectures, podcasts, and more all in one place. Instead of going through stacks and stacks of CDs, you can import them into iTunes and quickly browse your whole collection. Organize it any way you want. Move it onto any of your devices. Play it whenever — and wherever — the mood strikes. Even have Genius make mixes of songs that go great together.

Organize

With iTunes, it’s easy to get all your music organized. When you import your CDs, iTunes automatically adds the album, artist, and song names, along with the album art. Then, with your whole collection to choose from, you can create the ultimate playlists for rocking out, working out, or chilling out. You can also rate your songs with one to five stars, so it’s easy to find your favorites later.

iTunes Store.

The apps, books, podcasts, and lectures you want. And so many more you didn’t even know you wanted.

There are hundreds of thousands of apps, games, and podcasts you can download from the iTunes Store. And iTunes U contains lectures and videos created by top institutions around the world. It all adds up to a world of entertainment right at your fingertips.1

Family Sharing. Being a family has its perks.

Family Sharing makes it easy for up to six people in your family to share each other’s app purchases. Whenever a family member buys a new app, everyone else can download and enjoy it. And for kids under age 13, or any other family members you choose, you can turn on Ask to Buy, which requires parental permission before purchasing.

Learn more

Your music goes where you do.

It’s easy to keep all your iOS devices ready to rock. Open iTunes, click Settings, and choose music to see a list of what’s already there. To add something, click Add To, and your entire iTunes library appears next to the list of what’s on your device. Drag and drop. And you’re done.
