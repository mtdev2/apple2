iTunes.Your music and films
take centre stage.

iTunes is the best way to organise and enjoy the music and films you already have — and shop for the ones you want. Enjoy all the entertainment iTunes has to offer on your Mac and PC.

50 million songs. Zero ads.

Stream over 50 million songs, ad-free. Or download albums and tracks to listen to offline. All the music in your personal iTunes library — no matter where it came from — lives right alongside the Apple Music catalogue. Start your free, three-month trial with no commitment, and cancel anytime.


Apple Music is available in iTunes, and for iOS and Android devices.

Learn more about Apple Music

Turn wherever you are into your very own screening room.

With so many films to choose from, there’s always something great to watch on iTunes. And if you watch on Apple TV 4K, you’ll be able to enjoy a tremendous selection of your favourite content in 4K HDR. So get ready to enjoy the hit films you’ve been waiting to see — anytime, anywhere. Just tap to play, or even download if you’re going somewhere you won’t have Wi‑Fi.

A world of entertainment.
Available wherever you are.

The iTunes Store is available on all your devices, which means you can buy that catchy song you just heard or rent that film you’ve been meaning to see, anytime you want. And since your entire library of music and films lives in iCloud, everything you buy is immediately available, no matter how you want to play it.

Family Sharing.
Share your favourites with your favourites.

Family Sharing makes it easy for up to six people in your family to share each other’s iTunes purchases. Whenever one person buys a new film, everybody in your family can download and enjoy it. Families can share songs and albums too. And for kids under 13, or any other family members you choose, you can turn on Ask to Buy, which requires parental permission before purchasing.1
